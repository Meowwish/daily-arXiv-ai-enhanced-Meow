*Peisi Huang,Anibal D. Medina,Carlos E. M. Wagner*

Main category: hep-ph

TL;DR: 暗物质通过暗U(1)规范对称性与标准模型耦合，其湮灭截面通常被压低导致残留密度过高。研究发现，暗希格斯场的强一级相变可在暗物质冻结后通过熵注入机制降低残留密度，从而解释观测到的暗物质丰度。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在暗U(1)规范对称性破缺的暗区模型中，暗物质与标准模型通过动力学混合相互作用。由于小的动力学混合参数和/或重媒介子，暗物质湮灭截面通常被压低，导致残留密度过高，难以解释观测到的暗物质丰度。需要寻找新的机制来解决这一矛盾。

Method: 提出暗希格斯场在暗物质冻结后经历强一级相变的机制。暗物质丰度首先在对称相中通过热冻结过程确定，随后相变产生的熵注入降低了残留密度。通过计算相变参数和暗物质质量的关系，确定能够解释观测丰度所需的相变条件。

Result: 研究发现，为重现观测到的暗物质丰度，所需的相变通常是过冷的。这种强过冷相变产生的随机引力波信号位于未来实验的探测范围内，为这一框架提供了互补的探测手段。对于质量低于约10 GeV的暗物质，强过冷相变还可能解释NANOGrav观测到的信号。

Conclusion: 暗希格斯场的强一级相变为暗U(1)暗区模型提供了一种新的暗物质丰度调节机制。该机制不仅解决了小动力学混合参数导致的残留密度过高问题，还产生了可探测的引力波信号，为暗物质和早期宇宙相变研究开辟了新的探测途径。

Abstract: We consider a dark sector consisting of fermionic dark matter (DM) charged under a broken dark $U(1)_D$ gauge symmetry, interacting with the Standard Model through kinetic mixing. In such models, the DM annihilation cross section is typically suppressed by the small kinetic mixing and or a heavy mediator, often leading to an overabundant relic density. We show that the observed DM abundance can be achieved if the dark Higgs undergoes a strong first order phase transition after DM freeze-out. In this scenario, the relic abundance is set by thermal freeze-out in the symmetric phase and subsequently reduced by entropy injection from the phase transition, rather than by annihilation in the broken phase. We find that to reproduce the observed relic abundance, the required phase transition is generically supercooled. The resulting stochastic gravitational wave signal lies within the sensitivity of future experiments, providing a complementary probe of this framework. Moreover, a strongly supercooled phase transition can potentially account for the NANOGrav signal for DM masses below $O(10)$ GeV.

Such a mediator could, in principle, offer a novel pathway toward addressing one of the principal outstanding inconsistencies of the Standard Model: the nature and origin of dark matter.

### [11] [X-ray Spectral-Timing Properties of Tidal Disruption Events](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16868)
*Joheen Chakraborty,Megan Masterson,Andrew Mummery,Erin Kara,Christos Panagiotou,Riccardo Arcodia,Vera Berger*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 首次系统研究TDEs的分钟到小时尺度X射线随机变异性，发现含冕TDEs比纯热源变异性更强、PSD更陡，类似X射线双星状态转变，但新生TDE冕比AGN冕PSD更平、能谱更软。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究潮汐瓦解事件（TDEs）中X射线光度的分钟到小时尺度随机变异性，以探测超大质量黑洞周围新形成吸积流的微观物理特性，并比较TDE冕与活动星系核（AGN）冕的差异。

Method: 使用XMM-Newton数据和傅里叶分析方法，对18个TDEs的54次观测进行系统研究。测量了光谱特性、功率谱密度（PSDs）、分数变异性振幅以及变异性的能量依赖性，其中27次观测处于热盘主导状态，27次显示非热硬X射线冕。

Abstract: We perform the first systematic study of the minute-to-hours-timescale stochastic variability observed in the X-ray luminosity of tidal disruption events (TDEs) using XMM-Newton data and Fourier analysis methods. We measure the spectral properties, power spectral densities (PSDs), fractional variability amplitudes, and energy dependence of the variability for 18 TDEs spanning 54 observations, of which 27 occur in thermal disk-dominated states and 27 show a nonthermal hard X-ray corona. Compared to pure thermal sources, we find TDEs with coronae are more X-ray variable and show steeper PSDs indicating longer correlation timescales. This state-transition behavior is qualitatively similar to X-ray binaries, which show higher fractional variability in the hard state than in the soft state. However, newborn TDE coronae show systematically flatter PSDs and softer energy spectra than their long-lived AGN counterparts. We also show that the variability amplitude of thermal TDEs increases with photon energy, consistent with variations sourced by local temperature fluctuations and exponentially enhanced in the Wien tail. Our work demonstrates that combining spectral and timing properties of X-ray TDEs can probe the microphysics of newly formed accretion flows around supermassive black holes, and that the coronae formed in TDEs fundamentally differ from those in AGN.

### [12] [The spectral state transition of Mkn 590, a potential link between AGNs and X-ray binaries?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.17572)
*B. Palit,A. Rozanska,A. G. Markowitz,D. Lawther,M. Vestergaard,J. J. Ruan,T. Saha,G. Walsh,A. Borkar,M. Sniegowska,K. X. Lu*

Method: 使用Swift天文台长达十年的多波段观测数据，追踪X射线响度参数αox与爱丁顿比λEdd的关系，分析硬度-强度图上的不同吸积状态，并将结果置于黑洞活动基本平面上进行比较

Together, our results establish Mkn 590 as a rare, time-resolved case of AGN state transitions and offer compelling evidence for scale-invariant accretion physics across the black hole mass spectrum.
